What is Bahalina?

Bahalina is a traditional Filipino alcoholic drink made from fermented coconut sap, commonly known as tuba, and is especially popular in the Visayas, particularly Leyte and Samar. Aged in earthen jars, it develops a rich, reddish-brown color and a smooth, slightly sweet flavor that makes it a distinct part of local culture and celebrations.

Health benefits of Bahalina

When consumed in moderation, Bahalina may offer small health-related benefits due to its natural fermentation. It can contain probiotics that support gut health, trace minerals from coconut sap, and antioxidants produced during fermentation. However, these benefits only apply to moderate drinking, as excessive alcohol consumption can negate any positive effects.
